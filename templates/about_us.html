Mt team utility

Thanks for visiting my site!

My name is Carlo and I created this piece of software for my friends to use.

Over the years I have been part of a number of football groups and getting organised has always been a challenge.

Different groups have used all sorts of methods for organising games and to try to determine who is available for each game.

In addition, I've always felt it would be nice to gage how you compare as a player in the eyes of your peers, both from a core skills perspective and on a performance by performance basis

This app attempts to address these issues and more! Create or join groups, make matches, check each members availability, generate teams without an individual having to pick fair ones. Rate each player's skills and performance, see what newcomers look like and what to call them before kicking a ball, it's all here!

The design and features of the site will continue to be updated so stay involved for future improvements!

I hope you enjoy using myteam and find it useful!
